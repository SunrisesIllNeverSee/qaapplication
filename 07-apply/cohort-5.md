What drove you to this idea? How do you know that there is a burning need for what you are building?

600 characters max
I'm an avid gamer. I wanted video-game-style stats for AI users. Leaderboards, ranks, performance metrics. That was the whole idea.
To build it, I had to answer one question: what are you actually measuring inside a private AI session? Every existing benchmark measures the model in isolation. Nobody measures what the human operator is doing. So I went looking for the substrate; the thing rich enough to carry that signal. The only candidate is language itself. I applied thermodynamics and laws of conservation to language.
