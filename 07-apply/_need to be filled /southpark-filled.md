### What primary problem space(s) are you pursuing and why is it important?

AI systems have no memory across sessions, no accountability for past decisions, and no internal self-constraint against drift. Every current solution is either pre-execution (policies, training, guidelines) or post-execution (logs, audits). Nobody enforces governance at the moment the action fires.

This matters now because production AI work and agent commerce are arriving in 2026. Teams will waste enormous time and money on systems that look good in benchmarks but break when it counts -- because nobody measured whether the original intent survived the transformation.

I am also pursuing the consumer angle: there are no video-game-style performance stats for AI operators. No leaderboards, no ranks, no way to know if you are getting better. That is SigRank. The measurement layer that governs enterprise systems is the same substrate that powers the consumer product.
