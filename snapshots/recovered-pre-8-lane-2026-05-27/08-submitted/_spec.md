# 08-submitted — Spec

**What this lane is:** Immutable record of every submitted application. Sealed on submission.

**In the app:** Submission history. Users can view what they sent, when, and to whom. Never editable after submission (append-only).

**What lives here:** One folder per submission (`<program>-<batch>/`), containing `draft.md` (the working version) and `final.md` (clean copy of what was actually submitted).
